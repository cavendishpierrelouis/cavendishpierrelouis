I. Effective information & overview

Effective Date: November 11, 2025
Website: cavendishpierrelouis.io
Operated by: Cavendish Pierre-Louis


This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected during your visit, how that data is used, and the limited circumstances under which it may be shared.

This site was built as a professional portfolio and digital workspace. It does not operate as a commercial platform, does not require account creation, and does not engage in targeted advertising. All analytics are strictly performance-based and anonymized.

II. Information Collected Automatically


When you access cavendishpierrelouis.io, certain technical information is automatically processed to ensure functionality, security, and performance. This may include:

  • Browser type, device model, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation flow
  • Referring URL or search term used to reach the site
  • Approximate geographic region (city-level accuracy only)

This information is gathered through Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and is used exclusively to understand aggregate visitor behavior — no personally identifiable data (PII) such as names, emails, or IP addresses are stored in a retrievable format.

III. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies


Cookies are small data files placed on your device to improve site performance and analytical accuracy. This website uses only essential and performance cookies, which may include:

  • _ga / _gid — used by Google Analytics to distinguish sessions and measure usage statistics.
  • _gat — used to throttle analytics request rates.

Cookies do not collect sensitive information or track users across other websites. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to access the site.

IV. Legal Basis for Processing


If you are located in the EEA, processing of non-personal data through analytics is based on legitimate interest in maintaining and improving website performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)). Consent is not required because data is anonymized, limited in scope, and used solely for technical optimization.

V. Data Retention


Analytics data is retained for the minimum duration necessary to assess trends, typically no longer than 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted per Google’s retention policies. No manual export, storage, or profiling is performed.

VI. Third-Party Services


This site integrates with:

  • Google Analytics 4 — aggregated usage metrics
  • Cloudflare — DNS management and site-level security

Each service processes limited technical information under its own policy. No advertising pixels, remarketing tools, or social tracking scripts are active.

VII. External Links


Links to platforms such as Instagram, LinkedIn, Fiverr, or Amazon are provided for professional reference. Those sites operate under their own privacy terms.

VIII. Data Security


The site is served over HTTPS with TLS. Security and caching are managed through Cloudflare. No personal information is collected through forms or stored in any database.

IX. User Rights


Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request information about non-personal data held, ask for correction/deletion (if applicable), or object to analytics tracking.

Correspondence: hello@cavendishpierrelouis.io

X. Children’s Privacy


This website is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). If information is discovered from a minor, it will be deleted.

XI. International Access


By visiting from outside the U.S., you acknowledge data may be processed in jurisdictions with different protections. Data is never shared or sold.
