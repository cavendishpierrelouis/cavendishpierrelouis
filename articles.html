Why I Built This

<Hello World>,

by now you probably know what I do best: I love structuring the web. My first “site” was a basic page I coded at 8 years old that literally just said, “I love coding.” I’m still that kid, just with adult money now to buy the tools I want.

I built this blog to actually share what’s been sitting in my head and in my notes for years—real research, experiments, and patterns from shipping sites, not just reading docs.

These articles are for founders, studios, and teams who care how their site feels to use, not just how it looks in a single screenshot.

Sit back, scroll a little, and enjoy my take on the internet and how to build things that actually look good and work.

<Happy Coding Everyone>
